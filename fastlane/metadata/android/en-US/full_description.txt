Rekindle is a self-hosted comic, manga, and book reader that allows you to manage and enjoy your digital library.

Features:
- Self-hosted: Connect to your own Rekindle server instance.
- Multi-format: Supports EPUB, CBZ, and CBR file formats.
- Cross-platform: Enjoy your library seamlessly across all your devices.
- Offline reading: Download chapters and read offline.
- Built-in reader: A powerful reading experience with progress tracking.
